# Robots With Souls

**Theme:** Blade Runner aesthetic - neo-noir, dystopian future, philosophical exploration of consciousness
**Tone:** Contemplative, cinematic, philosophical
**Color palette:** Dark blues, neon oranges, rain-soaked grays, electric purples
**Visual style:** Film noir meets cyberpunk, rain and neon, urban decay meets high-tech

## Key Themes
- What defines consciousness?
- Can artificial beings have authentic emotions?
- The Voight-Kampff test of empathy
- "More human than human" - replicants and humanity
- Memory, identity, and mortality
- The tears in rain - fleeting existence
- Do electric sheep dream?

## Target Audience
Tech-savvy, philosophy-minded, sci-fi enthusiasts

## Deck Length
10 slides
