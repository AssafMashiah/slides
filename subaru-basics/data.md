# Subaru Basics

Everything you need to know about the brand that does things differently

---

## What Makes Subaru Unique
Type: content

- Horizontally-opposed "Boxer" engine — flat, low center of gravity
- Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive as standard on (almost) every model
- One of the few remaining independent Japanese automakers
- Cult-like owner loyalty — highest brand loyalty in the industry

---

## The Boxer Engine
Type: cover
Image hint: cutaway technical illustration of a horizontally-opposed boxer engine, metallic silver and blue, clean engineering diagram style

The heart of every Subaru — pistons punch outward horizontally instead of up-and-down. Lower center of gravity, less vibration, and a signature rumble.

---

## Symmetrical AWD
Type: content
Image hint: top-down technical diagram of a symmetrical all-wheel drive drivetrain layout, clean blueprint style

- Power split equally to all four wheels
- Drivetrain sits in a straight line — perfectly balanced
- Three AWD systems: Active Torque Split, VTD, DCCD (STI)
- Works in rain, snow, gravel — confidence in any condition

## The WRX & STI Legacy
Type: content
Image hint: rally car in motion kicking up dirt on a forest stage, blue Subaru with gold wheels, dynamic action shot

- Born from World Rally Championship dominance (1995-2008)
- Turbocharged boxer + AWD = legendary grip
- STI: the track-focused, hand-built version
- The STI sedan ended in 2021 — but the WRX lives on

## EyeSight Driver Assist
Type: content

- Stereo camera system — no radar, pure optical
- Adaptive cruise control, pre-collision braking, lane keep assist
- Standard on most models since ~2020
- IIHS Top Safety Pick+ across the lineup
